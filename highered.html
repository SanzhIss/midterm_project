CONDITIONS FOR ADMISSION TO THE MASTER'S PROGRAM

The persons who have finished the higher educational institution on programs of the bachelor degree can arrive on training in a magistracy. Training in a magistracy is free of charge under the state educational order (grant) and on a paid basis. Citizens of Kazakhstan can enter the magistracy under the grant only once, i.e. the second master's education will be only for a fee. Foreigners can count on education also on a paid basis, if the grant is not defined by international treaties. Specialties are advertised through the media 15 days prior to the start of the application process. Reception of necessary documents and applications for admission to the magistracy takes place from July 10 till July 30 in the higher educational establishments (HEIs) in which training of magistracy is conducted. The site has a service with a complete database of universities and masters programs, which will help you choose: where to enroll.

CONDITIONS OF ADMISSION TO THE DOCTORAL PROGRAM

Admission to the doctoral program is carried out for the educational programs approved by the central authorized body in the field of education, according to the branches of science. Doctoral studies are admitted to:
  • persons with an academic degree of "master";
  • Persons with higher specialized education;
  • Persons with an academic degree of "candidate of sciences".
