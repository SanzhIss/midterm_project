Preschool education

The educational process in preschool organizations regardless of the form of ownership and departmental subordination is carried out in accordance with the law: - The State Compulsory Standard of Preschool Education and Training (hereinafter - the Standard) (Order of the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan No. 604 dated October 31, 2018); - Standard curricula of preschool education and training № 557 of December 20, 2012 (as amended and supplemented on October 10, 2018 № 556); - Standard curriculum of preschool education and training (hereinafter - the Program) (Order of the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan dated August 12, 2016 № 499); - Typical rules for the activities of preschool organizations (order of the Ministry of Education and Science of the Republic of Kazakhstan dated October 30, 2018 № 595). The standard specifies the requirements for the level of preparation of children of preschool age, the content of preschool education and training, and the maximum amount of training load. The content of preschool education and training is based on the educational areas of "Health", "Communication", "Cognition", "Creativity" and "Socium", which are oriented to the diverse development of children, taking into account their age and individual characteristics and the integration of the content of educational areas.

The programme is a tool for educators and parents in the socialization of young children.
